📚 How to Read Decimal Dip Readings (Interpolation)

The chart lists volumes for whole centimetre readings only. For decimal dip readings, use the DIFF column to calculate the exact volume.

FORMULA Volume = Volume at lower cm  +  (decimal part × DIFF value)
